# AI Business Recommendations Engine

TradeSovereign v4 actively thinks about how to improve your product. The **AI Directives** tab reads your ecosystem performance metrics and offers distinct business scaling paths.

## How it works:
1. The platform utilizes a background Gemini contextual parser.
2. It analyzes student engagement dropoffs, API billing trajectories, and content scarcity.
3. Every 24 hours, it posts actionable strategic cards.

## Example Directives
- **Content Gaps:** "You uploaded PDFs for Physics but haven't generated Quizzes. Students drop off 34% faster without interactive checks. Action: [Click to Auto-Generate]."
- **Cost Minimization:** "Alpha Vantage API is being hit twice per second by identical IPs. Implementing a Redis layer saves $400/month. Action: [Deploy Edge Caching]."

*The platform ceases to be a passive website and becomes a proactive business partner.*
